About Us

Learn more about the team behind Trip Planner

Our Mission

At Travel Planner, we believe that traveling should be hassle-free and enjoyable. Our mission is to help travelers plan their perfect trips with personalized recommendations, efficient itinerary management, and innovative tools that enhance the travel experience.

Our Story

Travel Planner was founded in 2025 by a group of travel enthusiasts and CS major college student who were frustrated with the complexity of planning trips across multiple platforms. We set out to create a comprehensive solution that combines the best aspects of travel planning into one intuitive application.

As a college program at New York University, Travel Planner is quickly gaining recognition for its user-friendly interface and innovative features. We thrive with the support of our professors in the Cloud Computing program, TA, and Mentor. We receive direct support from AWS for development and maintenance.

Our Values

At the core of our company are these fundamental values:

  • Innovation: We are constantly striving to improve our platform with state-of-the-art cloud computing technology, integrating AI technology to provide a customized experience.
  • Reliability: We provide accurate, LLM based information that travelers can depend on. We have leveraged the features of AWS to build a platform that can support high concurrency and elastic computing.
  • Inclusivity: We design our app to be accessible for all types of travelers. UI design incorporates inclusive design to accommodate different needs.
